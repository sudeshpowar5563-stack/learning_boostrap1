FAQ stands for Frequently Asked Questions. It is a compilation of common questions and their corresponding answers that are often asked by customers or users. Having a FAQ section on a website, after a product description or in any customer support platform is crucial for various reasons.

A well-organized FAQ section in your brand voice can significantly reduce the number of repetitive inquiries received by businesses or organizations. Customers can find answers quickly without having to contact support staff, saving both their time and the company's resources.

Having a comprehensive FAQ section demonstrates that an organization values its customers' needs by providing them with easily accessible information. When customers can find solutions to their problems independently, they feel empowered and satisfied with the service provided.
